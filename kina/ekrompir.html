Baked Potatoes with Cheese

Wash the potatoes under running water. Do not peel. Cut into a fan shape, but do not cut all the way through. Use the handle of a spoon as a base, placing it under the potato. Place the cut potatoes in a baking tray lined with parchment paper. Melt the butter, but let it cool for a while to thicken. Add salt, pepper, garlic powder, chopped parsley, and basil. Brush each potato with the prepared mixture and bake at 200 degrees Celsius for about 30 minutes. After that, remove from the oven, brush again with the remaining butter, and sprinkle each potato with chopped cheddar cheese. Bake for another 30-40 minutes.
