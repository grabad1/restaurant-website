Banana Rolls

Roll out puff pastry dough and cut into strips. Place a banana on each strip and roll it up. Seal the edges with water. Deep fry the rolls until golden brown. Remove and drain excess oil on paper towels. Serve hot with powdered sugar or chocolate syrup.
